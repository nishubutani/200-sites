Floor Plans

Hill Country Classics Custom Homes has a large selection of Texas home floor plans with footprints from 600 to 2,500 square feet. If you have ideas or plans of your own, our staff designer specializes in creating custom homes in the Texas Hill Country and can help you put your ideas on paper. HCC will work to make your ideas function with the most efficient use of space to create a unique living space for you.

You can view floor plans for each of our Texas custom homes. Many of the homes also have alternate floor plans featuring revised custom designs.
